# Section Prompt: Testimonial Section

---

## Generation Scaffold

```
Generate a testimonials section for [product name].

**Design system:**
- Blueprint: [chosen]
- Palette: [chosen]

**Layout:** static grid ([2 / 3] columns) — NO carousel

**Testimonials:**
[Provide 2–4 real or realistic placeholder testimonials]

Testimonial 1:
- Quote: "[specific, outcome-focused quote — not 'This product is amazing!']"
- Name: [real-sounding full name]
- Title: [specific job title, company — e.g., "Head of Engineering, Stripe"]
- Avatar: use initials in a rounded neutral badge

[repeat]

**Anti-patterns:**
- No carousel or slider — all testimonials visible simultaneously
- No auto-play
- No star ratings unless the product genuinely collects star ratings
- Quotes must be specific and outcome-focused, not generic praise
- No gradient card backgrounds

**Output the complete testimonials section.**
```

---

## Testimonial Quality Standard

A good testimonial quote is specific:
- **Bad:** "This product changed everything for us. Highly recommend!"
- **Good:** "We cut deployment time from 45 minutes to under 3 minutes after switching. Our on-call rotations are 60% quieter."

If generating placeholder testimonials, make them specific. Generic praise testimonials communicate nothing.
